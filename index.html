About Me

I am a graduate of S1 Informatics Engineering from Tadulako University. In the course of my studies, I succeeded in achieving a Grade Point Average (GPA) of 3.76 with a study period of 3 years and 7 months, which shows my dedication, time management and hard work in completing assignments and mastering course material. During my studies I was also actively involved in organizational activities on campus. I am an active student in the field of organizations and have experience as General Chair of the Student Activity Unit of the Faculty of Engineering, Tadulako University (TECHNOSPORT). In this role, I was able to lead and manage the institution well. Apart from that, I also have experience as an assistant lecturer in Data Structure, Database and Analysis and Algorithm Design courses. As a teaching assistant, I help provide good understanding to students and support the learning process in class. This role expands my knowledge and skills in the field of informatics engineering and improves my communication and collaboration skills with lecturers and other students. These accomplishments reflect my commitment to academic excellence, leadership, and positive contributions within the campus environment. I have a high enthusiasm for continuing to learn and develop, and am ready to face new challenges in my career in the future.
